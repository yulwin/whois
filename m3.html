U Nyi Pu

U Nyi Pu is the NLD chair for his native Rakhine State.He graduated with a legal degree from Rangoon University in 1983 and then worked as a security guard until 1988.He briefly served as a clerk in the Land Transport Department and in the 1990 elections won but was not allowed to assume his parliamentary seat.In November's election,he won a spot in Gwa.His appointment to the chief minister post has been hotly contested by the Arakan National Party.
